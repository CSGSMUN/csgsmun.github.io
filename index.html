We are CSGS

The Computer Science Graduate Society (CSGS) has been established to represent the graduate students of the Department of Computer Science at Memorial University of Newfoundland. All currently registered students, either full-time or part-time, in any graduate program at the Department of Computer Science at the Memorial University of Newfoundland are considered members of the society.

Enhancement
