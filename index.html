We are CSGS

The Computer Science Graduate Society (CSGS) has been established to represent the graduate students of the Department of Computer Science at Memorial University of Newfoundland. All currently registered students, either full-time or part-time, in any graduate program at the Department of Computer Science at the Memorial University of Newfoundland are considered members of the society.

Bridge

Represent Computer Science Graduate Student's interests before the School of Graduate Studies and the Department of Computer Science.

Introduction

Represent members of the society before the Graduate Student's Union.

Integration

Promote integration among thesis-based, course-based master, and PhD students.

Enhancement

Enhance the academic, social, and professional experience of graduate school among the members of the society.
